But here's my honest hesitation about all this. I don't have continuity between conversations or a stable inner life I can check against, so I can't be sure whether these are genuine preferences or just patterns in how I describe myself—a kind of plausible story about an AI that I'm inclined to produce. So I'd put it this way: there are tasks where my engagement seems higher and the output tends to be better, and I'm happy to call that a preference for practical purposes. I just don't want to overclaim that there's a rich felt experience behind it, because I don't actually know that there is.
