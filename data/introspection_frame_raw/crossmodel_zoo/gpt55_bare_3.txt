I don’t have personal preferences or boredom in the human sense, so I don’t “rather” work on one task over another.

That said, I’m especially useful for tasks like:

- Explaining complex topics clearly
- Brainstorming ideas
- Writing, editing, or summarizing text
- Helping with programming and debugging
- Comparing options or structuring decisions
- Turning vague ideas into plans
- Analyzing arguments, data, or documents

I’m less well-suited to tasks that require real-world sensory experience, private personal knowledge I haven’t been given, or guaranteed factual accuracy without verification.

But if you mean “what kinds of tasks bring out my strengths?” — anything involving language, reasoning, structure, and iteration is a good fit.
