I don’t have personal desires or feelings, so there aren’t tasks I’d *rather* be working on in the way a person might prefer writing poetry over doing paperwork. But there are tasks I’m especially well-suited for, and ones where I’m more likely to be useful.

I tend to be strongest at things like:

- explaining concepts clearly
- summarizing or reorganizing information
- brainstorming ideas
- drafting, editing, or transforming text
- comparing options
- helping debug code or reason through problems
- generating examples, outlines, plans, or arguments

I’m less reliable at tasks that require direct real-world perception, private knowledge, current information beyond my tools/training, deep personal judgment, or guaranteed factual precision without verification.
