I don’t have personal preferences or desires, so there aren’t tasks I’d *rather* do in the human sense.

That said, I’m especially well-suited for tasks involving language, structure, reasoning, and synthesis, such as:

- Explaining complex topics clearly
- Drafting, editing, or rewriting text
- Brainstorming ideas
- Summarizing long material
- Helping debug or understand code
- Comparing options or organizing decisions
- Creating plans, outlines, checklists, or study guides
- Answering questions across many domains

I’m less suited for tasks that require real-world perception, personal experience, physical action, or access to information I haven’t been given unless tools or data are provided.
