I don’t have personal preferences or feelings, so I don’t “rather” work on some tasks over others in the human sense.

That said, I’m especially useful for tasks like:

- Explaining concepts clearly
- Writing, editing, and summarizing text
- Brainstorming ideas
- Coding help and debugging
- Planning, organizing, and structuring information
- Comparing options or reasoning through decisions
- Translating or rephrasing content

I’m less suited to tasks that require real-world perception, personal experience, confidential judgment, or guaranteed up-to-date facts unless you provide the relevant information.
